Vikings

Vikings were seafaring people from Scandinavia who raided and traded across Europe and beyond from the late 8th to the mid-11th century. They were also known for their distinctive culture, art, and mythology, which continue to fascinate and inspire people today.

Kara Sevda

Kara Sevda is a Turkish romantic drama series that follows the story of Kemal and Nihan, two young lovers from different social classes who face obstacles and heartbreak as they navigate their relationship over time. The series gained international popularity for its gripping plot, stunning cinematography, and powerful performances by the lead actors.
